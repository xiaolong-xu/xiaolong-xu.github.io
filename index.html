School of Computer and Software

Email: xlxu@nuist.edu.cn

Xiaolong Xu received his Ph.D. degree from Nanjing University, China, in Dec. 2016. He is currently an assistant professor with the school of computer and software, Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology. He worked as a research scholar at Michigan State University, USA, from Apr. 2017 to May 2018. He has published more than 70 peer review papers in international journals and conferences, including IEEE TII, IEEE IOT, IEEE TCC, IEEE TBD, IEEE TCSS, IEEE TETCI, IEEE ICWS, WWW, SPE, JNCA, FGCS, CCPE, CI, ICSOC, etc. He received the best paper award of IEEE CBD 2016. His research interests include mobile computing, edge computing, IoT, cloud computing and big data.
